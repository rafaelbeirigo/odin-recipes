Description

Butterbeer is the quintessential wizarding drink, enjoyed by Hogwarts students on trips to Hogsmeade. Frothy and comforting, it carries a rich butterscotch flavor with a hint of spice. Served warm in winter or chilled in summer, this Muggle-friendly version recreates the cozy feel without needing an enchanted barrel.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cream soda (chilled or lightly warmed)
  • 2 tbsp butterscotch syrup (or caramel syrup)
  • 1 tbsp unsalted butter
  • Â½ cup heavy whipping cream
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • Pinch of cinnamon (optional)

Steps

  1. In a small saucepan, melt the butter and whisk in the butterscotch syrup until smooth.
  2. Stir in the cream soda gently; warm it through if desired.
  3. Whip the heavy cream with vanilla until soft peaks form.
  4. Pour the drink into mugs, top with whipped cream, and sprinkle with cinnamon.
