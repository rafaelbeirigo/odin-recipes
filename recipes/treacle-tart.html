Treacle Tart

Image credit: Tony Hall from London, UK, CC BY 2.0, via Wikimedia Commons.

Description

Treacle Tart, Harry Potter’s favorite dessert, is a Hogwarts feast staple. Its filling is made with golden syrup (a British caramel-like sweetener), fresh breadcrumbs, and lemon. The result is a gooey, sweet, and slightly tangy tart that pairs beautifully with cream.

Ingredients

  • 1 9-inch pre-baked tart shell (shortcrust pastry)
  • 1 ¼ cups golden syrup (or substitute with light corn syrup + honey)
  • 2 tbsp fresh breadcrumbs
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • Zest of 1 lemon
  • 1 egg (beaten)

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Warm the golden syrup until pourable.
  3. Mix in breadcrumbs, lemon juice, zest, and beaten egg.
  4. Pour filling into the tart shell and spread evenly.
  5. Bake 25–30 minutes, until set and lightly golden.
  6. Cool before serving with cream or whipped topping.
